Alleviate

Let AI figure out your food triggers

Food-related symptoms often go undiagnosed for too long.
Our app is here to change that.

Vision

Alleviate is a valuable tool for those managing food sensitivities, enhancing well-being and helping to prevent adverse health effects. By tracking your meals and symptoms, our app uses a powerful AI to quickly identify food-related triggers, empowering you to make informed dietary choices.

All you need to do is fill out a quick daily survey on which foods you consumed and which symptoms you experienced - then the Alleviate does to rest! It provides you food recommendations based on the patterns that it detects from your data, and will finally let you know which allergies and intolerances may be behing your symptoms.
